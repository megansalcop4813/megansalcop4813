Understanding Chemistry

Chemistry is a branch of science that studies matter, its properties, and how substances interact to form new materials. Chemistry is used in medicine, energy production, environmental science, and everyday life.

Key Concepts in Chemistry:

  • Atoms and Molecules: The fundamental units of matter
  • Chemical Reactions: Describe how substances combine or break apart
  • Periodic Table: Organizes elements based on properties and atomic structure
  • Stoichiometry: Quantifies the relationships between reactants and products
