Understanding Biology

Biology is a branch of science that studies living organisms and the processes that sustain life. Biology helps us understand genetics, ecosystems, and the mechanisms that allow life to adapt and evolve.

Key Concepts in Biology:

  • Cells: The basic units of life
  • Genetics: The study of heredity and DNA
  • Evolution: Explains how species change over time
  • Ecology: Examines relationships between organisms and their environments
