Understanding Algebra

Algebra is a branch of mathematics that uses symbols and letters to represent numbers and quantities in formulas and equations. Algebra is used extensively in fields like computer science for algorithm design, finance for investment calculations, or architecture for structural design.

Key Concepts in Algebra:

  • Variables: Symbols (such as letters) that represent unknown or changing values
  • Expressions: Combinations of variables, numbers, and operations
  • Equations: Mathematical statements showing that two expressions are equal
  • Functions: Relationships between inputs and outputs
