Understanding Physics

Physics is a branch of science that studies matter, energy, and the laws that govern their interactions. Physics is used to explain how the universe behaves, and how to create technology and solve problems using it.

Key Concepts in Physics:

  • Newton’s Laws of Motion: Explain how forces affect movement
  • Energy and Work: Describe how energy is transferred and transformed
  • Waves and Light: Explore the behavior of oscillations and electromagnetic radiation
  • Thermodynamics: Studies heat, temperature, and energy efficiency
