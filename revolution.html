Understanding Industrial Revolution

The Industrial Revolution marked a major shift from handcraft to machine production. It transformed economies, societies, and lifestyles around the world through innovation and industry.

Key Concepts in Industrial Revolution :

  • Technological Advancements: Inventions like the steam engine and textile machines
  • Urbanization: Movement from rural areas to growing industrial cities
  • Economic Change: Rise of factories, trade, and global markets
  • Social Impact: Labor reform, class shifts, and new living conditions
