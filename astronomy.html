Understanding Astronomy

Astronomy is a branch of science that studies celestial objects, space, and the universe as a whole. Astronomy helps us understand the origins of stars, planets, and galaxies.

Key Concepts in Astronomy:

  • Solar System: Includes the Sun, planets, moons, and smaller bodies
  • Stars and Galaxies: Massive systems that shape the visible universe
  • Cosmology: Studies the origin, structure, and evolution of the universe
  • Telescope Observation: Allows scientists to study distant celestial occurences such as planets
