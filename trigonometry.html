Understanding Trigonometry

Trigonometry is a branch of mathematics that studies the relationships between the angles and sides of triangles. Trigonometry is used in engineering, physics, architecture, and even video game design to model movement and waves.

Key Concepts in Trigonometry:

  • Sine, Cosine, and Tangent: Fundamental trigonometric ratios that relate angles and sides
  • Unit Circle: Represents all possible angle values on a coordinate plane
  • Trigonometric Identities: Equations that simplify complex trigonometric expressions
  • PI: A special number that represents the unchanging ratio of a circle's circumference
